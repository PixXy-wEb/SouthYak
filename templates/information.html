Origin story

Started in a dorm room

South Yak started in a cramped dorm room during finals week. Our founders—three college students tired of watching shows alone—realized that TV could be more than just passive entertainment. It could be a shared experience, a study tool, a way to stay connected.

"We built the product we wished existed. Something that combined our love for TV with the reality of college life." — Alex, Co-founder

What began as a weekend project quickly became a mission. We realized that thousands of students felt the same way: frustrated with complicated setups, missing their friends, and craving a better way to watch. Today, South Yak is trusted by over 10,000 students across the country.
