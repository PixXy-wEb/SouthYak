FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How do I create a South Yak account?
+
Click "Get Started" in the top right corner. Enter your email, create a password, and verify your email. Students can verify their .edu email for 50% off.
What devices are supported?
+
South Yak works on any device with a modern browser: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge on Mac, Windows, Chromebooks, and tablets. Casting works via Chromecast, AirPlay, or HDMI.
How do I get the student discount?
+
Sign up with your .edu email address. We’ll send a verification link. Once verified, you’ll automatically see student pricing (50% off Premium).
Can I watch with friends who don't have South Yak?
+
Watch parties require all participants to have a South Yak account. However, friends can sign up for free with basic features.
How does Study Mode work?
+
Study Mode lets you take timestamped notes, save clips, and search transcripts while watching. Perfect for film studies, media analysis, or language learning.
Why is my stream buffering?
+
Check your internet connection. We recommend at least 5 Mbps for HD streaming. Try lowering the video quality in settings or restarting your router.
How do I cancel my subscription?
+
Go to Account Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription. Your subscription remains active until the end of the billing period. No hidden fees.
Is my data safe?
+
Absolutely. We never sell your data. All streams are encrypted. We’re a dedicated team that values privacy and kindness.
Can I use South Yak on my TV?
+
Yes! Cast from your browser using Chromecast or AirPlay. You can also connect via HDMI cable for the best quality.
What's the refund policy?
+
We offer a 14-day free trial for all paid plans. If you’re not satisfied, cancel anytime during the trial with no charge.
